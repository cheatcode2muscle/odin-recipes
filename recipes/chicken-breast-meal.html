The Ultimate Jim Bro Meal

Description

This guide will teach you to make the Ultimate Jim Meal to buff you and your cat up.

List of ingredients

  • Chicken Breast Boneless Skinless:Approximately 200g
  • Rice:Approximately 1 cup(158g)
  • Brocoli:Choice of amount.(More veggies wont hurt)

Steps to make Ultimate Jim Bro Meal

  1. Cook rice in a rice cooker the asian way.
  2. Boil a pot of water.
  3. Place chicken breast into the boiled water and cook for 10 minutes with stove heat off.
  4. Place brocoli into the same pot of chicken breast when the chicken breast have been cooke for around 7 minutes.
  5. Take out both brocoli and chicken breast when 10 minutes have been reached and serve with cooked rice.
  6. Finish the meal you gotta fuel up for DEM GAINZ.
