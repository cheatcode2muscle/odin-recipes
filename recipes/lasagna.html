Lasagna

Description

We will teach you to make the best lasagna that even your cat would fight over you for it.

List of ingredients

  • Meat:Any meat of choice
  • Onion and Garlic:An onion and 2 gloves of garlic.
  • Tomato Product:Can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Spices and Seasoning:Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna Noodles:Store brought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheese:Any cheese of choice.
  • Egg:An egg.

Steps to make Lasagna

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 50 minutes in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

Steps to layer the Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Cheese slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Shreded cheese
  7. Repeat the list and top with remaining shreded cheese
